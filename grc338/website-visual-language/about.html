Born from the shared dream of a group of friends from different corners of the Mediterranean, our restaurant is a celebration of heritage, friendship, and the flavors that brought us together. What began as late-night conversations around home-cooked meals grew into a commitment to create a space where everyone can feel the warmth and generosity of a Mediterranean table.

Each of us arrived with unique stories, family recipes, and culinary traditions passed down through generations. Together, we blended these influences—Greek, Turkish, Italian, Lebanese, Spanish, Moroccan, and more—into a menu that honors our roots while embracing the creative spirit that emerges when cultures meet. Our dishes reflect the diversity of our backgrounds and the unity we've built over many shared meals.

Beyond the food, our restaurant was created as a place of connection. We wanted to build a home away from home, where strangers become friends and friends become family. Every detail—from the music to the décor to the way we plate each dish—was chosen to evoke the comfort and joy of being welcomed into someone's home.

At the heart of our kitchen is a belief that food is a universal language of love. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a lingering dinner, we hope you feel the care in every ingredient, the stories behind every recipe, and the spirit of friendship that brought this dream to life. Welcome to our table—there's always a seat waiting for you.
